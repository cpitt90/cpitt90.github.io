What I Do Best.

01 Enterprise UX

I make software that people in finance, healthcare, and regulated industries actually understand how to use. My work goes into products where users need to trust what they're doing.

02 Research & Strategy

I watch how people actually use products. I talk to teams about what needs to change. Then I make sure everyone agrees on what we're building before we build it.

03 Design Systems

I create a playbook for how your product looks and works so everyone on the team builds the same way. This saves time and keeps things consistent.

04 Product Thinking

I ask the tough questions. Does this solve what customers actually need? Are we building the right thing? I make sure users have a voice in decisions.
